Mayzeek Middle School

Albert E. Meyzeek Middle School (commonly referred to as Meyzeek Middle School or simply, Meyzeek) is a public middle school, with grades 6-8, in the Smoketown neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky, just southeast of Downtown Louisville. Meyzeek is part of the Jefferson County Public Schools, one of the largest school districts in Kentucky. The school is on 828 South Jackson Street, and has 1120 students enrolled as of 2007.[1] Mr. Chris Burba is the principal of Meyzeek Middle School.
