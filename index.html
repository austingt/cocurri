All of your school's clubs & events - in one place.

This single application gives school administrators access to a dashboard of all the clubs, events and activities within a school. Administrators can easily view calendars, rosters and any other needed information about co-curricular activities happening within their school at any time.

Easily manage multiple clubs

Teachers can use the application to manage club activities by sharing information, calendars, dues collection, or sending group messages to students and parents. They can manage registrations, milestones, deadlines and any other factors involved in overseeing clubs, academic competitions and events.

24/7 Access to the Activity Calendar

CoCurri gives parents and students 24/7 access to the co-curricular activity calendar. They can view current information regarding deadlines, dues, and more. The tool lets students submit forms, documents, registrations, dues and anything else required for an event or club. The calendar feature promises that parents and students will no longer miss deadlines or meetings.
